We are preparing the upcoming release of The Science of Code v2, a brand-new version of our open-source community.

The Science of Code is a website where people can share knowledge about computing for free and without any kind of censorship.

We've decided to remove all ads and tracking systems, so the new website will be 100% privacy-focused. The new version will be available in English and Spanish from day one.

While we finish it, you can check the old version, which is currently available only in Spanish:

https://thescienceofcode.azurewebsites.net/ 

Keep coding!
